About School LES RAMPARDS

School LES RAMPARDS is one of the first training institutions for digital professions in Congo. Our mission is to train a new generation of digital professionals capable of meeting the technological challenges of Africa and the world.

We offer intensive, hands-on programs designed in collaboration with industry experts to ensure our students acquire the most sought-after skills in the job market. Our innovative pedagogical approach combines theory and practice, with a particular focus on real projects and internships.

At the School of Congo, we don't just train developers and digital experts, we train the future leaders who will shape the technological future of Congo and Africa.
